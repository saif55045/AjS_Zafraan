Menu Aj'S Zafraan Limited

Do you like that delightful Indian flavour--- complete with buttery naan and spicy flavour-rich Biryani? Zafraan 3 and 1 has it all! How about some Italian? Tasty pasta and savoury pizzas galore are available too! But, what if none of the former tickle your fancy? That's okay! How would you relish in a fresh steaming jaw-dropping Turkish kabob at your doorstep? Zafraan has several options that could fit the needs of any picky family, and that is why it is called "3 and 1." There are also many Western options too, which would be perfect for travellers missing and looking for a taste of home. Among those western options is southern style fried chicken, burgers, and wraps that show up to your door steamy and mouth-watering and ready to be enjoyed by you. Even if you have special dietary needs, for instance, vegetarians can relax and know that there are several options available. These options include a select vegetarian pizzas, vegetable biryani, fresh garden salad, vegetable kabab, and vegetable pakora. On the opposite spectrum, let's say you are a meat lover. We have several options for you too!
